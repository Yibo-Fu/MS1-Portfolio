Day4: Space Travel without Gravity (1)

Entertainment. Mahjong in space. This is a re-design to the traditional Chinese Mahjong game for space travel without gravity. This design aims to make it possible to let you feel the same way as it should be on earth when playing Mahjong blocks. I use magenet to let those blocks stick to the table surface so that you can still throw those blocks hard onto the table to make big sound(which is a tradition to show/fake regret/excitement during the game), just like what you can do with Mahjong on earth.
