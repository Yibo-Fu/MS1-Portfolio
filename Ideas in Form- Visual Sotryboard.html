Ideas in Form: Visual Storyboard

Two ideas are demonstrated:

The first idea is to use the six sides of a cube to show six different universal mechanical structures. However, the feedback might not be so intuitive.

The second idea is just like the Russian nesting doll, a series of universal mechanical structures are used to open themselves and to reveal the inside one in order to make audiences being curious about figuring out how to open the next one.

Confirmed universal mechanical structures to be used:
1. A 'spinning bottle cap' that can be opened clock-wisely.
2. A button that cannot be pressed but the rest of the surface can be pressed.
